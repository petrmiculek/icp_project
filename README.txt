Application simulating public transport vehicles traveling around town.
Vehicles spawn at their start stop at a given time and despawn at their last stop when they reach it.
Spawn times, vehicle's route and stops it stops at are saved in examples/ as json files.

Vehicles movement is possible to fast forward or slow down. It is not possible to reverse time.
Every vehicle waits at a bus stop for a certain amount of time.

User can modify the traffic density on a certain street, from no traffic to full on traffic jam.
